Cocktail Shaker Sort: Like bubble sort, but we bubble the max up and then the min down each time. Keep going until no swaps made. 

def cocktailSort(array){

    startInd = 0
    endInd = array.length - 1


    while madeSwap:

        for (int i = startInd, n = endInd; i < n; i++) {
            if array[i] > array[i+1]{
